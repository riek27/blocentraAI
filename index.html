Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about BlocentraAI

How does BlocentraAI's insurance work?

Our insurance uses smart contracts to provide automatic coverage based on AI risk assessment. When you connect your wallet, our algorithms evaluate risk factors and provide real-time coverage that adjusts as market conditions change.

Is BlocentraAI non-custodial?

Yes, our core product is non-custodial, meaning you retain full control of your private keys and Bitcoin. We also offer custodial solutions for institutional clients who prefer that model.

What makes your AI different from others?

Our AI is specifically trained on Bitcoin transaction data, market patterns, and security threats. It incorporates machine learning models that continuously improve with more data, providing unparalleled risk assessment accuracy.

How are claims processed?

Claims are automatically processed through smart contracts when predefined conditions are met. Our AI verifies claims in real-time, and payouts are typically processed within hours rather than weeks.

Is BlocentraAI regulated?

We are pursuing regulatory licenses in multiple jurisdictions including USA, Switzerland, and Singapore. Our compliance framework is designed to meet global standards for insurance and financial services.
