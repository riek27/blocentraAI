Problem & Solution

The problem

Despite Bitcoin's massive valuation and adoption, it remains underprotected and underutilized:

  • Over 99% of BTC sits idle, with no protection or yield
  • No native insurance standard for BTC wallets or transactions
  • Retail users lack wallet security and behavioral risk detection tools

  • No AI-native solution exists to forecast market events or detect anomalies
  • Traditional insurers lack tools to assess decentralized risk

  • Non-custodial and custodial protection options

Frequently Asked Questions

How does BlocentraAI's insurance differ from traditional crypto insurance?

BlocentraAI offers Bitcoin-native, behavior-driven insurance powered by AI. Unlike traditional crypto insurance which often requires custody or focuses on exchanges, our solution is designed specifically for Bitcoin's architecture with dynamic pricing based on real-time risk assessment.

Is BlocentraAI a custodial service?

No, our core offering is non-custodial. Users maintain control of their private keys while benefiting from our protection services. We also offer custodial solutions for institutions that prefer managed services.

How does the AI risk scoring work?

Our AI analyzes multiple data points including wallet activity patterns, transaction history, threat intelligence feeds, and market conditions to generate dynamic risk scores that update in real-time.

How long does it take to activate coverage?

Coverage can be activated instantly after risk assessment. Our AI completes the risk evaluation in seconds, enabling immediate protection for your Bitcoin assets.
