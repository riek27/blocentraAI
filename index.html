The Problem

Despite Bitcoin's $1.2T+ market cap, critical infrastructure gaps remain:

  • 99% of Bitcoin sits idle with no protection or yield
  • No native insurance standard for wallets or transactions
  • Retail users lack security tools, institutions face compliance hurdles
  • Existing solutions are Ethereum-centric or custodial
  • Risk management is reactive rather than predictive

Our Solution

BlocentraAI delivers Bitcoin-native risk infrastructure:

  • AI-driven behavioral analysis & automated underwriting
  • Non-custodial design preserving user ownership
  • Modular API platform for seamless integration
  • Predictive models for systemic risk forecasting
  • Compliance tools for institutional adoption
